CAD

For the CAD unit we made a Skateboard in onshape.

Making the Deck

I started out by making a fully constrained sketch using the center rectangle and circle tools. Then I extruded my sketch, put bolt holes in the board to mount the trucks and filleted the boards edge.

Making the Trucks

Making the trucks was very interesting to me because we made parts in the air before connecting them. One thing I learned is that you can use a sketch after you made it by using the use tool, this is very helpful because you don't need to remake constraints that you made before.
